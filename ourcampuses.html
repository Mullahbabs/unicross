Accross Unicross

The University of Cross River State (UNICROSS) operates a multi-campus system, strategically positioned across different locations within Cross River State. Each campus contributes uniquely to the university's mission of delivering quality education, fostering innovation, and promoting community development. Here’s an overview of the various campuses:

Despite their diverse specializations and locations, all campuses of UNICROSS are united by a shared commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and community service. Together, they embody the university's vision of providing accessible, inclusive, and impactful education to students across Cross River State and beyond.

Below is an overview of Our various campuses.

Calabar Campus (Main Campus)

The Calabar Campus, located in the state capital, serves as a vibrant hub for academic and administrative activities. It houses key faculties such as Management Sciences, Environmental Sciences, and Arts & Humanities. The campus boasts modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, and an enriching learning environment, making it a focal point for intellectual and cultural exchange.

Visit


Obubra Campus

Situated in the serene town of Obubra, this campus focuses primarily on Agricultural Sciences and Natural Resources Management. It provides students with access to expansive farmlands and research facilities, enabling practical, hands-on learning experiences. The campus is pivotal in promoting sustainable agricultural practices and environmental conservation.

Visit


Ogoja Campus

The Ogoja Campus specializes in Basic and Applied Sciences, offering a strong foundation in disciplines such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. The campus fosters a robust academic environment that encourages research, innovation, and the pursuit of scientific knowledge.

Visit


Okuku Campus

The Okuku Campus is renowned for its programs in Education and Educational Foundations. It plays a vital role in training future educators and professionals who will contribute to shaping the educational landscape. With a focus on pedagogy and teacher education, the campus equips students with the skills and knowledge to excel in the teaching profession.

Visit


Ikot Ekpene Satelite Campus

As a developing addition to the UNICROSS system, the Ikot Ekpene Satellite Campus is focused on expanding the university’s reach and access to quality education. It offers a growing selection of programs aimed at meeting the specific needs of students in the region.

Visit
