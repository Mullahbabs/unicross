About Campus

The Calabar Campus of the University of Cross River State (UNICROSS) serves as the flagship campus of the institution, located in the vibrant heart of Cross River State’s capital city. It is the administrative hub and a center of academic excellence, offering a diverse array of programs across multiple faculties.

These faculties offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to prepare students for leadership roles in their chosen fields while addressing the needs of society through innovative research and practical solutions.

Facilities & infrastructure

The campus boasts modern lecture halls, well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and ICT centers to provide a conducive learning environment. Its urban location ensures access to various amenities and opportunities for industry collaboration and community engagement.

Students Life On Campus

The Calabar Campus fosters a vibrant student community with a range of extracurricular activities, cultural events, and professional development opportunities. Students benefit from a rich blend of academic rigor and social experiences, ensuring a well-rounded university life.

Commitment To Excellence

As the central campus of UNICROSS, the Calabar Campus is committed to upholding the university’s mission of delivering quality education, fostering innovation, and promoting sustainable development. It serves as a gateway for students to achieve academic success and contribute meaningfully to society.
