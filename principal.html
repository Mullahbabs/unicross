Our Principal Officers

The Principal Officers of the University of Cross River State (UNICROSS) are the key leaders responsible for upholding the university’s mission and ensuring its efficient and effective governance. These officers represent the highest levels of administrative and academic authority within the institution, providing strategic leadership to drive its growth, innovation, and overall success.

Composition Of Principal Officers

The Management Team is led by the Vice-Chancellor and includes other principal officers who oversee key aspects of the university’s operations. These members typically include:

The Vice-Chancellor.
The chief executive and academic officer of the university, the Vice-Chancellor provides visionary leadership, oversees policy implementation, and ensures the institution’s mission aligns with national and global standards of higher education.
The Deputy Vice-Chancellors (DVC's)
Supporting the Vice-Chancellor, the DVCs are responsible for specific portfolios such as academics, administration, and research, ensuring the smooth execution of strategic initiatives.
The Registrar.
Serving as the head of administration, the Registrar manages the university’s records, coordinates administrative processes, and acts as the Secretary to the Governing Council, Senate, and other key bodies.
The Bursar.
As the chief financial officer, the Bursar is tasked with overseeing the university’s finances, ensuring fiscal discipline, and maintaining accountability in resource allocation and expenditure.
The University Librarian.
The custodian of the university’s intellectual resources, the Librarian manages the library and its services, ensuring access to knowledge and supporting research and learning activities.

Roles & Responsibilities

The Principal Officers collectively ensure the smooth operation of the university and its alignment with established policies and objectives. Their responsibilities include:

Strategic Leadership
Steering the university’s direction and fostering an environment conducive to academic and professional excellence.
Governance & Policy Implementation
Translating policies into practical strategies that advance the university’s mission.
Resource Management.
Overseeing financial, physical, and human resources to maximize efficiency and productivity.
Stakeholder Engagement
Building relationships with internal and external stakeholders, including government agencies, donors, alumni, and the community.
Quality Assurance
Ensuring the delivery of high-quality academic programs and administrative services.

Significance In University Governance

The Principal Officers are the pillars of governance at UNICROSS, ensuring that the university operates in compliance with its statutes and regulations. Through their leadership, they foster a collaborative and innovative environment that supports the institution’s goals and enhances its reputation as a leading center of learning and development.

Commitment To Excellence

The Principal Officers of UNICROSS are dedicated to upholding the university’s core values of integrity, inclusivity, and excellence. Their unwavering commitment to good governance and transformative leadership continues to drive the institution toward achieving its vision of empowering students, fostering innovation, and contributing to societal advancement.
